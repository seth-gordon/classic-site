A Policy Wonk's Alphabet of Dreams

At the dawn of the twenty-first century, the human race sinks into a cultural war that one writer called "Jihad vs. McWorld". On the one side, government-sponsored sexism, suicide bombers, and lies; on the other, multiple-choice education, property rights über alles, and lies.

In the hope that our species is capable of more than that, I hereby offer some alternative lies, from my travels in countries of the imagination.

  • A is for Anonymous: Come to the place where nobody needs to know your name.
  • B is for Beautiful: How much is your face worth to you?
